FAQ

Our team is always striving to provide the best support to help you with any questions related to conference attendance and to make information more transparent and instantly accessible, we've started gathering most frequently asked questions on the page below.

If you can't find an answer to your question below – contact us via hi@jsnation.com.

Actions with tickets

  • Do you provide refunds of remote conference tickets and workshop tickets if decided not to participate?

    No, we do not provide refunds in this case due to our policies.

  • Can you issue an invoice for my order?

    Yes, it is possible to pay via invoice. Please, send your company details to hi@jsnation.com and we will issue it for you:

    • company name
    • company address
    • VAT number.

    As soon as we receive the payment, we'll send you a promo-code to activate the tickets.

Program

  • Where can I find the full schedule?

    The full schedule will be published a few weeks before the event on the website but you can find some speakers on the website already.

  • Where can I find all logistics and organisational information on how to connect, what the exact schedule is, etc.?

    You will get an email with all the information you’ll need at the conference about 2-3 days before the event.

    Please note, the email will contain access code to the conference activities.

  • What are the Managed Q&A sessions after each talk?

    It’s a 10-15 minutes session right after each talk when MC picks questions from the audience and asks them to the speaker.

  • How can I ask questions to the speakers during Managed Q&A?

    All the questions can be asked via Slack channels. The links to the Slack will be shared in organisational email 2-3 days before the event.

  • What is 1:1 Advice Lounge?

    It’s a virtual meeting with experts whom you can ask questions about your projects. Registration with the list of available experts and their schedule will be open in advance, and you will be able to book a slot for discussion with any expert.

    The details will be placed on the website.

  • What after-party will include?

    There will be numerous afterparty rooms with different hosts and activities from chat rooms to Quake tournament.

  • Where to find the links to zoom rooms, slack channels?

    All the links will be placed on the streaming website. To enter a zoom room or any other activity, you’ll have to put your access code from the organisational email.

  • What is the timing of the conference?

    The conference will run on June 18-19 from 3pm CEST, until around 7pm (core part).

  • What are the Speakers personal discussion Zoom rooms?

    It’s a virtual discussion room with a speaker that will be open after a managed Q&A session. The links will be placed on the website with streaming.

  • How to communicate with other participants?

    The main slack channels (one per day – General + Backend & Testing) will be created – the links to Slack will be sent in organisational email before the conference.

  • What are the Discussion zoom rooms focusing on particular technologies?

    During the day there will be a number of discussion rooms where participants can chat, share experience and knowledge on multiple tech topics. Each room will be active during one hour.

  • Where I can find the list of 3h workshops?

    The full list of 3h workshops will be published a few weeks before the event on the website.

Technical questions

  • How to install Zoom?

    You can use the web version of the program, install zoom on a computer or mobile phone (App Store, Google Play).

    You can enter Zoom by using your Google account, Facebook or Zoom account (if you have already registered with this program). If you do not have a Zoom account, click Register to create one.

  • What to do if the stream, zoom rooms don’t work?

    If the stream doesn't work, please update your page first. If you still have any issues please contact us via hi@jsnation.com or in Slack channel.

About JSNation Live

  • What does JSNation Live (June 18-19) consist of?

    • 2-days live stream with 1 track per each day: General + Backend & Testing
    • Managed Q&A sessions after each talk
    • Speakers zoom rooms – there you can chat with speakers after their talks and Q&A sessions
    • Networking zoom rooms focusing on multiple tech topics: GraphQL, Node.js, React, Vue.js, Angular, etc.
    • Slack for networking and question for speakers’ talks
    • 1:1 Advice Lounge with experts whom you can ask questions about your projects
    • A number of after-party zoom rooms with different activities and QuakeJS Tournament
    • Random watching party – you can join random group of people in watching/discussing conference together

Tickets

  • If I have a ticket to an in-person JSNation conference can I attend JSNation Live for free?

    Yes, if you have a JSNation ticket you’ll get full access to all the JSNation Live activities 3-4 days before the event.

  • Is it possible to watch the live stream for free?

    Yes, but you won’t be able to participate in all networking activities.

    Please note, to have access to the stream you have to register here.

  • What does free registration include?

    • Live Stream of 2 days: General + Backend & Testing
    • 720p streaming quality
    • Access to talks recordings 1 month after the event

  • What does a ticket with full access to JSNation Live include?

    • Full HD streaming quality
    • Free 3h workshops
    • Discussion rooms focused on particular technologies
    • Participating in Q&A sessions
    • A remote after-party with speakers
    • Quake tournament
    • Speaker personal discussion Zoom rooms
    • Advice lounges (1-1 with speakers)

Workshops

  • Does a full access ticket give the opportunity to join all the workshops around the conference?

    Please note there are two kinds of workshops:

    • 3h workshops which are included in a full access ticket
    • 8h workshops that should be purchased separately

  • Where can I find all the prerequisites I need for a workshop?

    3-4 days before the workshop you’ll get an email with all the instructions: how to connect to the remote workshop, what to install in advance, what the schedule is, etc.

    If you don't receive this email, contact us at hi@jsnation.com.

Discounts

  • Do you provide any group discounts?

    Yes, for teams from 4 people discounted tickets are available.

  • Do you have any discount for students?

    There are no discounts for students.

Other

  • We are a community/also a tech conference. Could we collaborate with your conference?

    Yes, of course! We’d be happy if you become our info-partner. Please, drop us a line at hi@jsnation.com and we’ll get back to you with all the information.

  • Will the talk recordings be available after the conference?

    The recordings will be available for full access tickets holders right after the conference and in a month after the event for the whole community on Youtube channel.

  • I didn’t receive any organisational email with access code, what to do?

    If you do not receive access code and the rest details 1 day before the event, please contact us at hi@jsnation.com.
