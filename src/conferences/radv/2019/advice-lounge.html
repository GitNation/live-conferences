    Advice lounge

    The conference is attended by some of the top people in the industry and core OS contributors of our beloved libraries. Would you like to get their advice on your work?

    If that’s the case, you can join our Advice Lounge, a new React Advanced discussion format held at the conference Lounge & Catering area. The lounge provides a laid-back setup open for your questions to advisers.

    They will be present at the spot during the coffee breaks, and there's a system for you to book time with specific people – click the "book a session" link under each expert to reserve a spot.

    Individual adviser main field of expertise is marked with tech icon below their bio.
