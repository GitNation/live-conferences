If you can't find an answer to your question below - contact us via events@gitnation.org.

Recordings

  • If you are a Multipass or Full ticket holder you'll get early access to talk recordings at gitnation.com.

    Note, the publication of the recordings from hybrid day can take around 1-2 weeks as they require more time for post-production. While the recordings from remote days are usually available the next day.

  • The recordings will be published on gitnation.com and will be shared with Full ticket and Multipass holders within 3-4 weeks.

Live Stream

  • The conference will be streamed on a separate watch page. You’ll get the instructions on how to access it via email the day before and one more email on the day of the conference.

    Alternatively, you can access the watch page from the main conference website. Check the website on the conference day – there will be button 'watch livestream'. Click it and follow the instructions.

Technical questions (Remote attendance)

  • Please check under which email you're trying to open the stream (should be the same email under which you have a conference ticket / Multipass / badge registration).

    If it doesn't work, please contact us here.

Discounts, diversity scholarships, students, etc.

  • Yes, there is a special price for students. Go to the conference website to the section with tickets, click 'Select your ticket', and check if there are any student tickets available on the checkout page. If there are no such tickets on sale then it means that they are sold out.

    We recommend that you hurry to buy tickets.

    Make sure to have your valid student ID at the entrance to the conference, you'll be asked to show it at the registration desk.

  • Yes, to get a group discount please click the ‘Order now’ button in the tickets section and choose “Team Ticket”.

    Please note that team tickets may be sold out, so it's best not to delay your purchase. Also, a team discount does not apply to Early Bird tickets as this is the lowest price we offer.

Workshops

  • Free Workshops are the complementary part of Full Tickets (Remote Full ticket and Hybrid Ticket). They will be run remotely via Zoom. The registration will be available via gitnation.com when we finalize the schedule of the workshops. You’ll get an email about that and the instructions on how to register 1 month before the first workshop.

    As per Pro Workshops, they are not included in Full tickets and should be bought separately. Pay attention that some Pro Workshops might be held in-person, and some – remotely via Zoom.
