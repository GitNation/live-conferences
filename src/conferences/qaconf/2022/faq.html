FAQ

About TestJS Summit 2022

  • What does TestJS Summit 2022 consist of?

    • Nov 3 – Live stream day with 2 tracks of talks, Q&As with speakers
    • Nov 4 – Discussion rooms
    • Managed Q&A sessions after each talk
    • Speakers personal video rooms – there you can chat with speakers after their talks and Q&A sessions
    • Networking video rooms focusing on multiple tech topics
    • Discord for networking and question for speakers’ talks
    • After-party video rooms with different activities and QuakeJS Tournament

Program

  • What is the timing of the conference?

    The conference will run on Nov 3 at 4 pm CET (10 am EST) until around 9pm CET (core part), Nov 4 at 4 pm CET (10am EST) until around 7 pm CET

Organisational info

  • Where can I find all logistics and organisational information on how to connect, what the exact schedule is, etc?

    You will get an email with all the information you’ll need at the conference about 2-3 days before the event & we'll duplicate the message on the day of the conference before the stream.

    If you didn't receive the email, please check your spam folder.

    And if you still haven't found the email, please contact us via events@gitnation.org.

Actions with tickets

  • If I can’t attend the conference, can I give the ticket to my friend/colleague?

    Yes, you can. Simply re-assign your ticket in your confirmation email you’ve received after registration. Pay attention that only a ticket buyer can re-assign tickets.

  • I’d like to buy a ticket. Can you issue an invoice for my order?

    Yes, it is possible to pay via invoice. Please, send your company details to events@gitnation.org and we will issue it for you:

    • company name
    • company address
    • VAT number
    • number and type of tickets
