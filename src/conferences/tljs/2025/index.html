The Conference for Tech Leads, Staff Engineers, and Technical Eng Managers

TechLead Conf 2025 tackles two critical challenges facing technical leaders today: navigating AI adoption in organizations and reducing system complexity. Through real-world case studies from startups to Big Tech, senior engineers and tech leads will share practical insights from the trenches.

  • September 18: Expert talks and live Q&A sessions
  • September 19: Themed discussion rooms for deeper dives

Join us remotely for both days to learn from industry practitioners who've solved these challenges firsthand.
The following topics will be covered:
  • AI in organizations
    • AI-powered development workflows
    • Interviewing Engineers in AI-era
    • Upskilling engineering teams
    • Cross-functional AI enablement
    • Rethinking Engineering Roles in the Age of AI Disruption
    • Ethical AI implementation
    • Measuring AI impact and ROI
  • Managing system complexity
    • Navigating Complexity in Agentic systems
    • Technical debt strategies and Impact of AI Tools
    • Architectural evolution at scale
    • Cognitive load and developer experience
    • Legacy system modernization
    • Third-party dependencies and supply chain risks
  • Eng. Career Growth Deep Dives
    • Follow our year-round TechLead Deep dives covering topics like:
    • Becoming an irreplaceable engineer
    • Growth into tech leadership roles
    • Staff Engineer + challenges
