IT IS NODE ROCKET SCIENCE

Join us remotely on April 4-5, 2024, for a comprehensive update on modern JavaScript backend runtimes, and best practices. Network with top industry engineers and open-source contributors, enhance your skills and find inspiration, friends, and new career opportunities.
This year we will cover the following Runtimes:
  • Bun
  • Deno
  • Cloudflare Workers
  • Node.js
  • Biome
  • Wasp
and issues from the surrounding topics:
  • Security
  • DevOps
  • Scaling
  • Case Studies
  • Serverless
  • Performance
  • Architecture

Matteo is an renowed international speaker after more than 60 conferences, including OpenJS World, Node.js Interactive, NodeConf.eu, NodeSummit, JSConf.Asia, WebRebels, and JsDay just to name a few. He is also co-author of the book "Node.js Cookbook, Third Edition" edited by Packt.

In the summer he loves sailing the Sirocco.
