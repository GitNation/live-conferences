JSNation for Team Leads & Managers

Would you like to your developers to skill up in JS and become more efficient at what they do? Help them attend JSNation US. Your company will profit in many ways with your employees’ newly gained knowledge, motivation, and network.

JSNation brings together 700 international JS experts in NY, both on stage and within its audience. Attendees get to learn from them not only during talks, but also during breaks and associated events.

With a full ticket, your developers will gain access to:

  • 50+ talks
  • 10+ complimentary workshops
  • Extended program with extra events
  • Networking with speakers and industry professionals online
  • Certification upon event or workshop completion

Connecting, chatting, and learning from top-notch talent in the industry can be incredibly inspiring. Your devs will be surrounded by a diverse group of people who have created and maintain the JS ecosystem and who work on resolving problems your devs have encountered. Your team members can return motivated and eager to apply their new learnings.

On top of that, they can:

  • Speed up the existing processes
  • Fill their knowledge gaps
  • Improve their code quality
  • Share key take-aways with the rest of the team
  • Write a report on the company blog
Sounds good? Help your developers to grow, and encourage them improve their skillset!
